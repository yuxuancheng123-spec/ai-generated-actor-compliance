    Control architecture

    The case study treats real-person face and voice data as high-risk inputs and places controls before generation, before publication, and after complaints.

    Gate

    Pre-generation review

    Identity verification, consent records, license scope, and prohibited-use screening.

    License

    Granular authorization

    Separate permissions for face, voice, motion, commercial use, duration, territory, and training.

    Label

    Disclosure and provenance

    Visible labels, metadata labels, watermarks, content credentials, export logs, and hashes.

    Respond

    Rights-holder playbook

    Complaint intake, rapid containment, evidence preservation, takedown, and control improvement.
